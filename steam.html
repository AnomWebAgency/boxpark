Steam

With more steam car wash branches than any other car wash company in Harare, we know steam!
In 2018 Box Parkbranched out to using steam clean technology at some of our full service car washes and since then, steam cleaning has become one of our most popular offerings.

Why Steam?

Steam uses less water per car which is environmentally friendly. We also use no chemicals with steam and are able to steam the inside of the car which you cannot do with a normal water wash, It also fully sanitizes which is a win!

Car seats and Pram Services

Box Park’s steam car washes also offer the cleaning and sanitizing of car seats and prams with minimal down time – this is a hugely popular service as our steam clean technology uses no chemicals so it is completely safe for babies and children.

Steam Services

Our steam car washes also offers a variety of services such as mini and full valets, engine cleans, polishes, leather and aircon treatments and more. We also stock a variety of air fresheners to keep your car smelling fantastic.

The Steam Team

Our steam car wash uses steam machines imported from Italy and Korea and our teams are constantly learning and given on-site training. Box Park has fully comprehensive insurance and liability cover should the need ever arise.

FAQs

Box Park is the only carwash company in Zimbabwe to offer water, steam and waterless services. We also offer a range of other services – valets, engine cleaning, leather treatments and the cleaning and sanitizing of car seats and prams and so much more.

Is steam clean technology good for the environment?
Steam clean technology is definitely good for the environment as it uses minimal water. This is in line with Box Park’s commitment to do our absolute best to protect the environment and scarce resources such as water. Steam uses no chemicals at all.
Is it safe to have my car seat or pram cleaned with steam?
Yes! Our steam clean technology uses no chemicals so it’s completely safe for babies and children. No more dirty car seats, prams and fabric high chairs with Box Park’s steam clean service.
Does steam sanitize?
Our steam clean offering is the perfect solution – especially during the times we are living in – steam sanitizes and disinfects your car and gets rid of any germs (as well as any dirt!)
How long does a full valet take with steam?
A full valet – which includes a wash, dry, vacuum, wheels, tyres, leather, polish, seats, carpets, boot & engine clean – can take up to two to four hours, depending on how dirty the car is and whether you have cloth or leather seats (cloth seats need additional drying time)
Do I have to book for a valet?
No, but it can get busy so we suggest coming first thing in the morning, if possible!
