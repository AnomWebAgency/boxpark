More Than
Just A Car Wash

Box Park Wash is all about creating the ultimate car wash experience for our customers. We pride ourselves on being more than just a car wash. Our driving force has always been to provide excellent service and constant innovation, while uplifting the local communities we work in and building a brand that can be trusted and loved!

Who we are

Born from the love of cars, Box Park Car Wash began in 2009, with the aim of creating a car wash brand that was so much more than just a car wash. Box Park is committed to providing a unique car wash experience for our customers, while offering incredible customer service. Through our passion for business, we continue to grow at an exponential rate, in a variety of sectors. Our mission is to be the leading car wash services company in Zimbabwe, while always being cognisant of the environment and job creation.   We are proud  to be the only car wash company in Harare to offer water, steam and waterless services. Box Park is really so much more than just a car wash!

The Box Park Family

At Box Park, we are more than a team, we are family. We are committed to creating jobs within the local communities we operate, and believe in investing in our people - their success is our success! What sets us apart from the competition are our pre-hiring processes, constant on-site training, upskilling and low staff turnover. Every member of our team has been fully trained in car-cleaning to an extremely high level. Be assured that every time you visit one of our branches, you’ll be met with the same service and care and will be treated with the utmost respect. Box Park is also proud to offer the best earning system in the industry, where our washers have the opportunity for excellent earning potential.

PRODUCTS & SERVICES

Box Park is the only car wash company in Harare to offer water, steam and waterless services. We also offer a range of other services – valets, engine cleaning, leather treatments and the cleaning and sanitizing of car seats and prams and so much more.
